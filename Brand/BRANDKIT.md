# ClipShot Brand Kit

## Brand Idea

**Precision in frame.**

ClipShot turns an exact screen region or web component into a finished visual.
The identity combines registration marks, measured drafting tools, and a quiet
native macOS workspace.

## Core Mark

The ClipShot mark is formed by two opposing registration corners:

- top-left corner: capture begins
- bottom-right corner: the finished frame
- negative space: the selected component or screenshot

Use the mark with generous clearspace. Do not enclose it in a camera, QR code,
scissors, or another literal screenshot symbol.

## Color

| Role | Token | Hex |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Drafting room | `Theme.canvas` | `#131110` |
| Graphite panel | `Theme.surface` | `#1D1A17` |
| Stage well | `Theme.stageWell` | `#0F0D0B` |
| Inset control | `Theme.inputFill` | `#231F1B` |
| Warm ivory | `Theme.textPrimary` | `#F5F1EA` |
| Warm gray | `Theme.textSecondary` | `#B0A79A` |
| Vermilion | `Theme.accent` | `#FF5C38` |
| Vermilion light | `Theme.accentText` | `#FF8E6B` |

Vermilion is reserved for selection, focus, registration marks, and the primary
action. Capture-derived colors may softly illuminate the stage, but should not
replace the core graphite and vermilion identity.

## Typography

- **SF Pro:** titles, labels, commands, and interface hierarchy
- **SF Mono:** dimensions, values, shortcuts, coordinates, and technical detail

Use sentence case and short labels. Keep the product voice precise and quiet.

## Visual Language

- warm graphite drafting surface
- measured dot grid and registration ticks
- capture-derived ambient light
- floating warm-tinted glass controls
- thin hairlines and restrained shadows
- one clear saturated action per surface

## Product Applications

- menu-bar companion
- native screen and window capture
- web-component selection overlay
- professional screenshot editor
- copy and save workflows

## Avoid

- generic camera or scissors imagery
- neon cyberpunk treatments
- purple AI gradients
- dense dashboard compositions
- decorative color that competes with the captured image
